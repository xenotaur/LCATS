# Evidence Record: EV-0002

## Method

The special-character pipeline (WI-RULES-0016 rules + WI-NORMALIZE-0017
gather-time hook + WI-OVERRIDES-0018 overrides + the WI-RESIDUAL-0019 classifier
refinement and allowlist) was evaluated by a **deterministic simulated
regeneration**: `lcats.gatherers.normalization.normalize_story_dict` was applied
in memory to every story body in the live `data/` tree (1,971 stories), then
`lcats.analysis.corpus.specials` classified and surveyed the result with the
seeded allowlist. This exercises the exact code paths a real `data/`
regeneration runs, without a ~2,000-story Project Gutenberg re-download. A
literal cache-clear + `lcats gather` regeneration is the maintainer's operational
step and will reproduce these numbers because the pipeline is deterministic.

## Observed Signals

- Current `data/` (gathered before the pipeline landed): **36 stories** carry
  raw mojibake markers.
- After the simulated regeneration: **0 `likely_repairable` (mojibake)
  findings** across all 1,971 stories (down from 24 residual occurrences before
  the classifier refinement).
- After applying the seeded allowlist: **2 residual flagged characters remain,
  both `review_needed` (non-mojibake)** — one stray `U+FEFF` BOM and one lone
  `U+25A0` BLACK SQUARE.

## Interpretation

The mojibake-cleanup goal is met: a regenerated corpus produces zero repairable
special-character findings, and legitimate characters no longer generate survey
noise. Every measured residual has an explicit, traceable disposition.

## Not addressed here (follow-up)

- `U+FEFF` stray BOM (1 occurrence, before a Gutenberg start marker) and
  `U+25A0` lone BLACK SQUARE (1 occurrence) are **boundary/typographic
  artifacts, not mojibake**, and were deliberately left unallowlisted so they
  keep surfacing for review rather than being masked. They belong to boundary
  cleanup, outside this workstream's encoding scope.

## Confidence

- High confidence that the deterministic pipeline yields zero mojibake findings
  on regeneration (same code path exercised over the full corpus).
- The literal full `data/` regeneration + `lcats survey --mode specials` run
  remains a maintainer operational step to confirm end-to-end.
